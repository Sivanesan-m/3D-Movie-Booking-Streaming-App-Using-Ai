Frequently Asked Questions

How does 3D movie booking work?

Simply browse our extensive catalog of 3D movies, select your preferred showtime and seats, and complete your booking in seconds. Our app provides real-time seat availability and instant confirmation for a seamless experience.

Can I stream movies at home in 3D?

Yes! Our premium subscription includes access to our 3D streaming library. You can enjoy immersive 3D content at home with compatible devices and 3D displays, plus book theater experiences for the ultimate cinematic adventure.

What devices support the app?

Our app is fully responsive and works seamlessly across iOS, Android, tablets, and desktop browsers. The 3D streaming features are optimized for devices with 3D display capabilities and VR headsets.

How do I cancel or modify my booking?

You can easily manage your bookings through the app. Cancel or modify your reservations up to 2 hours before showtime for a full refund. Premium members enjoy extended cancellation windows and priority rebooking.

Are there subscription plans available?

We offer flexible plans including monthly and annual subscriptions with benefits like discounted bookings, exclusive 3D content access, priority seating, and ad-free streaming. Start with our free tier and upgrade anytime.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing. Your financial data is protected with bank-level security, and we never store sensitive payment information on our servers.
